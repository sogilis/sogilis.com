Our purpose

Since 2008, Sogilis carries the ambition to advance the industrialization of software and products requiring a very high level of quality in order to respond to very high criticality issues both industrial and economic.

Whether it is the efficiency and quality expected by large and small industrial groups or creativity and innovation on the start-up side, thanks to our team of experts, we bring them pioneering methodologies and groundbreaking software creation workshops:

  • Support our customers towards more efficiency and quality with the development of their solution.
  • Creation of innovative methodologies and tools to reduce critical systems' development costs.
  • Cross-cutting vision on cloud native projects and tailor-made solutions for our customers.
  • Conception, development and marketing of software products that meet certified industries standards' challenges, such as aeronautics, health or banking.
